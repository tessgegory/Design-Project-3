All about softball

What is this website about?

This website is about softball and all of the rules that comes with the sport. It will explain the rules, my summer 2022 team, all about my association and a page about me.

Why did I choose this topic?

Softball is one of the sports I play, and it has brought a lot of joy into my life. It’s a sport that I love because I can have fun with my people while doing something that I enjoy. Softball is such a great sport, but not many girls play so this is a great way to show people why I love it and also explain a little bit about it.

How did I choose this topic?

I had many options of what I could make my website about, but after recently winning the provincials with my softball team, that was definitely the thing that finalized my decision. I have a lot of love for softball, so I am very glad that I ended up choosing it as a topic, and I hope that the viewers enjoy it as much as I do! Although it wasn’t originally going to be my choice, this is most likely the best subject that I could have created a website about.
